Welcome to D&A Building Services

A family of facility maintenance companies

As premier providers of commercial and industrial building services, these veteran-owned companies are comprised of D&A Building Services, Inc. and D&A Window Cleaning, Inc.

The companies serve public and private sector clients using their own staff, not subcontractors. Established in 1985, D&A employs highly trained individuals operating from offices from Florida and Texas.
